messageboard_page_handler (   $page)

Messageboard dispatcher for flat message board. Profile (and eventually group) widgets handle their own.

URLs take the form of User's messageboard: messageboard/owner/<username> Y's history of posts on X's board: messageboard/owner/<X>/history/<Y> New post: messageboard/add/<guid> (container: user or group) Group messageboard: messageboard/group/<guid>/all (not implemented)
